4 Ingredient Lemon Pound Cake

The secret to this 4 ingredient lemon pound cake is sweetened condensed milk. Dense but moist, it makes a
great base for strawberry shortcake. When served by itself, top it with a simple lemon juice and powdered
sugar glaze.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 4 teaspoons lemon zest (optional)
  • 1 1/4 cups self-rising flour
