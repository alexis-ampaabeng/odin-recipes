No-Bake Snickers Pie

This no-bake Snickers pie tastes just like the Snickers bar you know and love-caramel, peanuts, and chocolate, but in a fluffy, creamy pie. Roasted, salted peanuts in the graham cracker crust add delightful crunch and salt.

Ingredients

Crust

  • 7 ounces graham crackers (about 13 crackers) or 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup roasted salted peanuts
  • 1/4 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/3 cup creamy or chunky peanut butter
  • 1/2 cup jarred caramel sauce or dulce de leche
  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
